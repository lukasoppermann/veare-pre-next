swift / we are fast

  • #concept
  • #corporate design
2011
#corporate identity#branding

swift is an airline corporate identity focusing on the corporate values comfort, trust and speed.

The primary color yellow visualises speed and is mainly used in communication and marketing, while the dark green stands for comfort and calmness and is mainly used inside the airplanes & lounge areas.

Swift Logo Design

It took about 100 different variations of 6 names to find the one that was just right, a reduced Logotype that is bend to the right to give a feeling of movement and a rough resemblence of the shape of an airplane.

The logo did not only have to be trustworthy, but needed to fit wiht the target audience of mainly traveling businessmen.
